description: Check Pokémon compatibility for breeding.

Quickly check if your Pokémon are compatible for breeding and will generate an egg.
- Genders must be opposites
- Genderless Pokémon can only breed with Ditto, as long as their egg group is not undiscovered
- Ditto cannot breed with itself
- Manaphy cannot breed with anything except Ditto
